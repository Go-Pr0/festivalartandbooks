Jonathan Fawkes

My Precious Self

Incandescent martyr
Dying in the flames
Carrying the evil
Purged and freed from blame;
Mercy spent upon you
Harnessed wickedness
Out of cavern hidden
Sought your own redress.

Splintered, wretched villain
Two sides of one coin -
Slinker crept so quietly,
Stinker treason joined:
One talks to the other
Both talk to the One,
Precious, parasitic
For mind enslaved so long.

Murdered your own kinsman
To steal what he refused -
One more Birthday Present
Truth and guilt suffused.
Gave in to temptation,
Evil and the dark
Shadowed under mountains
Filled with riddling talk.

When you were abandoned
By the love you craved
Thoughts of wasted moments
That many lifetimes raved,
You began a journey
Coming face to face
With the fount of evil
But you still escaped.

Through a dwarven stronghold,
Down a river long
Followed through the sharp lands
Driven, twisted, wronged
Crossed the jagged rockscape,
Promised thieves your aid,
Through the marshes silent
Path so safely made.

Twisting stair and straight stair
Spiders trap that failed
Through the gasping desert
Precious burden trailed
Clambered up the mountain
Pity there repaid
Fell into that firestorm
My Precious self Unmade.
