Barbara Remington's hurried cover art for the 1965 Ballantine *Lord of the Rings* paperbacks
— produced before she had time to read the books — became one of the most recognisable
Tolkien images of the 1960s, even though Tolkien himself disliked it. Original pieces from
that commission rarely appear on the open market.

This is the original Remington illustration (not a reproduction), offered with a certificate
of authenticity. The drawing shows her characteristic gnarled tree, rolling hills, and
Hobbit-hole landscape in pen and ink wash. Serious enquiries only; full condition report and
provenance on request.
