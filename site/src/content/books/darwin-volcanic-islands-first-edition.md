Part of Darwin's geological results from the *Beagle* voyage, published by Smith, Elder in
1844 as *Geological Observations on the Volcanic Islands visited during the Voyage of H.M.S.
Beagle*. First editions in the original dark cloth binding are genuinely scarce; most copies
that surface have been rebound or heavily restored.

This is a first-edition, first-issue copy in the publisher's cloth, with the spine lettering
"Darwin on Volcanic Islands" still legible. Wear is visible to the spine and boards — as
photographed — but the text block is sound. A proper antiquarian piece outside Tolkien but
within the wider rare-book stock I carry.
