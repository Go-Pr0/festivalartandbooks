*Tree and Leaf* (1964) is the first book-form publication of Tolkien's Andrew Lang Lecture
"On Fairy-Stories" together with the allegorical short story "Leaf by Niggle". The Allen &
Unwin first edition is a slim octavo in the familiar pale green dust-jacket.

This copy is a true first impression in fine condition throughout — clean text block, tight
binding, and an unfaded dust-jacket with no tears or loss. A straightforward copy for
collectors building a run of Tolkien's minor hardcover firsts.
