The 1976 Allen & Unwin deluxe *Hobbit* was the first boxed, illustrated edition of the book in
this format: black cloth stamped in gilt and red with Pauline Baynes's Smaug roundel, housed
in a grey slipcase with a printed title label. It post-dates the first edition by nearly
forty years but is collected in its own right as the inaugural deluxe issue.

This copy is a first impression of that edition, book and slipcase complete. The binding is
clean, the gilt bright, and the slipcase shows only light shelf wear. Photographs show the
book and case together.
